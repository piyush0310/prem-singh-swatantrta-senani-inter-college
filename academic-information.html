Academic Information
Home    Academic information

At Prem Singh Swatantrta Senani Inter College, Dhampur, we are committed to delivering quality education that fosters intellectual growth, character building, and all-round development.

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to provide a strong academic foundation, incorporating modern teaching methodologies while adhering to the guidelines of the Uttar Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (UPBSE).

  • Primary Section: Classes 1 to 5
  • Middle Section: Classes 6 to 8
  • Secondary Section: Classes 9 and 10
  • Senior Secondary Section: Classes 11 and 12 (Science, Commerce, and Arts streams)

Subjects Offered

  • Primary & Middle Section: English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Computer Science, and General Knowledge.
  • Secondary Section: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Economics, History, and Civics.
  • Senior Secondary Section:
    • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
    • Commerce: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics
    • Arts: History, Political Science, Geography, Sociology

Teaching Methodology

  • Interactive and student-centered teaching techniques.
  • Integration of smart classes to enhance learning experiences.
  • Regular assessments, practical sessions, and hands-on projects.

Facilities

  • Well-equipped science and computer labs.
  • Comprehensive library with diverse learning resources.
  • Spacious and ventilated classrooms for a conducive learning environment.
  • Playgrounds and facilities for sports and physical education.

Extracurricular Activities

  • Debates, quizzes, and creative writing competitions.
  • Sports activities, including cricket, football, and athletics.
  • Cultural programs celebrating national and local heritage.
  • Leadership and personality development workshops.

Examination and Evaluation

  • Regular unit tests, term exams, and annual examinations.
  • Continuous Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) system for classes 1 to 8.
  • Emphasis on academic performance, attendance, and participation in activities.

Results and Achievements

Our students consistently achieve remarkable success in board examinations and actively participate in state-level academic and sports competitions.
